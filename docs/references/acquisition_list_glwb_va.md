## High Priority (Foundational)

### 1. Milevsky & Salisbury (2006)
- **Title**: "Financial valuation of guaranteed minimum withdrawal benefits"
- **Journal**: Insurance: Mathematics and Economics, 38(1), 21-38
- **DOI**: 10.1016/j.insmatheco.2005.06.012
- **Why**: Foundational GMWB pricing framework; most-cited paper in GLWB literature
- **Access**: ScienceDirect (paywall) or SSRN preprint
- **Status**: [ ] Not acquired

### 2. Milevsky & Posner (2001)
- **Title**: "The Titanic Option: Valuation of the Guaranteed Minimum Death Benefit in Variable Annuities and Mutual Funds"
- **Journal**: Journal of Risk and Insurance, 68(1), 93-128
- **DOI**: 10.2307/2678133
- **Why**: Death benefit valuation methodology; foundational for GMxB pricing
- **Access**: JSTOR or Wiley
- **Status**: [ ] Not acquired

### 3. Bauer, Kling & Russ (2008)
- **Title**: "A Universal Pricing Framework for Guaranteed Minimum Benefits in Variable Annuities"
- **Journal**: ASTIN Bulletin, 38(2), 621-651
- **DOI**: 10.1017/S0515036100015269
- **Why**: Unified framework for all GMxB types (GMDB, GMAB, GMIB, GMWB)
- **Access**: Cambridge Core OR free PDF at ressources-actuarielles.net
- **Status**: [ ] Not acquired

### 4. Huang, Milevsky & Salisbury (2014)
- **Title**: "Optimal initiation of a GLWB in a variable annuity: No arbitrage approach"
- **Journal**: Insurance: Mathematics and Economics, 56, 102-111
- **DOI**: 10.1016/j.insmatheco.2014.04.001
- **Why**: Optimal withdrawal timing; policyholder behavior modeling
- **Access**: ScienceDirect (paywall)
- **Status**: [ ] Not acquired

## Acquisition Strategy

### Option A: University Library Access
If you have university affiliation:
- ScienceDirect provides most Insurance: Mathematics and Economics papers
- JSTOR for older Journal of Risk and Insurance papers
- Wiley for Mathematical Finance

### Option B: Interlibrary Loan
- Request through local library
- Usually free, 1-2 week turnaround

### Option C: Author Preprints
- Check author websites or ResearchGate
- Many authors post preprints freely
- Milevsky has many papers on his York University page

### Option D: Purchase
- Individual articles: $30-50 each
- Books: $60-150 each
- Consider: Milevsky (2006) book is best single purchase
